1,3,7-trimethylurate

Alternative Names

  • Trimethyl uric acid
  • 8-oxy-caffeine
  • caffeol

Molecular Information

  • Weight: 210.19 [1]
  • Formula: C8H10N4O3 [1]
  • Classification: water-soluble uremic toxin [3]

General information

Trimethyluric acid is a methyl derivative of uric acid, found occasionally in human urine [2]. It is a metabolite of caffeine [1].

Clinical Implications

Trimethyluric acid is a uremic toxin which is known to have an association with the following diseases

Asthma

Colorectal cancer

Classification
  • Polar
  • Water Soluble uremic toxin
